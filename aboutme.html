About Me

My name is Anko Obeng. I am a 20-year old college student. who is a big fan of anime and manga. I have been watching anime since i was 7 years old. i made this website to help people find anime for them, because most people think of anime as childish cartoon shows. That is because most don't know about the many subgenres it has. I want people to find the genre for them, so that the negative connotations anime has declines.
