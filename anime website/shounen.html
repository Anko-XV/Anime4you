Shounen

On this page I explain behind what Shounen is and which shows then might be something for you. Shōnen manga is a Japanese comic book category for teenage boys. Shōnen manga is one of the main categories of manga, along with shōjo, seinen and josei manga. It is aimed at boys and is usually published in special magazines. Shōnen manga is the most popular category in Japan. Although it seems that it is mainly aimed at young men, people of all ages and genders read it. Shōnen manga originated in Japanese children's magazines in the early 20th century and became popular in the 1920s. It mainly focuses on action, adventure and fighting monsters or evil. Although action stories are the most common , there are many genres within shōnen manga, such as comedy, crime, romance, "slice of life" (everyday life literally translation piece of life) and sports. One Piece

Luffy, a young man made of rubber after accidentally eating a devil fruit, sets out on a journey from the Eastern Blue Sea to find the late pirate king Gol D Roger's treasure. As Luffy and his crew begin their adventure, others join the crew later in the series, Together, they encounter pirates, bounty hunters, criminal organizations, revolutionaries, secret agents, various scientists, soldiers from the morally ambiguous world government along with many other friends and enemies, as they sail the seas in pursuit of their dreams. The Navy is the maritime military branch of the World Government that protects the high seas from pirates and other criminals. The central tension of the series pits the World Government and its forces against the pirates. The series frequently emphasizes the moral ambiguity of the "pirate" label, which includes cruel villains, but also all individuals who do not submit to dictatorial rule - and often dream moral lake - of the world government. The One Piece world also has supernatural features such as Devil Fruits , which are mysterious fruits that give the power of transformation to anyone who eats them. The world itself is made up of two vast oceans divided by a giant mountain range called the Red Line In the oceans there exists a second global phenomenon called the Grand Line, which is a sea area perpendicular to the Red Line and surrounded by the calmbelt stretches of ocean The quiet is invaded by giant ships. eat monsters called Sea Kings Therefore, traveling between the four seas and the Grand Line is very difficult.

bleach

Ichigo Kurosaki is a teenager from Karakura Town who can see ghosts, a talent that allows him to meet Rukia Kuchiki, a supernatural being, who enters the town in search of Hollows, a type of monstrous spirit possessed by going missing can harm both ghosts and humans. Rukia is one of the Shinigami , soldiers responsible for guiding the souls of the dead from the world of the living to the Soul Society, the afterlife where she goes and fights Hollows. When she is seriously injured defending Ichigo from a Hollow she is pursuing, Rukia transfers her powers for Ichigo, so that he can fight in her place while she is thus trapped in her normal human body and must advise Ichigo as he balances his duties as a Soul Reaper replacement and his studies.To help them hunt the Hollows, the pair join forces with a trio of other powerful allies: Ichigo's classmate Orihime Inoue, close friend Yasutora "Chad" Sado and Uryū Ishida, a classmate from Quincy with the ability to control soul particles.

naruto

Moments before Naruto Uzumaki was born, a giant demon known as Kyuubi, the Nine-Tailed Fox, attacked Konohagakure, the Hidden Leaf Village, and wreaked havoc. To stop the Kyuubi's rampage, the village leader, the Fourth Hokage, sacrificed his life and sealed the giant monster inside the newborn Naruto. Now, Naruto is a hyperactive ninja still living in Konoha. Alienated because of the Kyuubi within, Naruto struggles to find his place in the village, while his burning desire to become Hokage of Konoha not only leads him to formidable new friends but also dangerous enemies.

Fullmetal alchemist:brotherhood

Brothers Edward and Alphonse Elric were raised by their mother Trisha Elric in the remote village of Resembool, in the country of Amestris. Their father, Hohenheim, a famous and highly gifted alchemist, abandoned the family when the boys were still young, and while in Trisha's care, they began to show an interest in alchemy and became so curious to know its secret. Returning home, the two decided to try to bring their mother back to life using alchemy. After Edward receives automatic prosthetic limbs from Winry and Pinako, the brothers decide to burn down their childhood home, symbolizing their determination and decision to “not go back,” and move forward. capital to become a state alchemist sanctioned by the government. After passing the exam, Edward is nicknamed "Fullmetal Alchemist" by the State Military, and the brothers begin their journey to regain their full bodies through the legendary Philosopher's Stone under the led by Colonel Roy Mustang.

Yuji Itadori is an unusually fit high school student living in Sendai. On his deathbed, his grandfather gave Yuji two powerful messages: “always help others” and “die among people”. Yuji's friends at the Occult Club attract the Curse to their school when they unseal a rotten finger talisman. Yuji swallows his finger to protect the Jujutsu witch Megumi Fushiguro, becomes the host of a powerful curse named Ryomen Sukuna. Due to Sukuna's evil nature, all witches must exorcise him (and by extension, Yuji) immediately. However, upon seeing Yuji maintain control of his body, Megumi's teacher, Satoru Gojo, takes her to Tokyo Prefectural Jujutsu High School with a proposal to her superiors: postpones Yuji's death sentence and trains under Gojo until he consumes all 20 of Sukuna's fingers so the curse can be lifted. At the same time, a group of cursed spirits prepares a multi-layered attack on the magical world of Jujutsu, including the cursed spirit Mahito and a corrupted magician named Suguru Geto, who was possessed by Gojo. executed a year earlier.
